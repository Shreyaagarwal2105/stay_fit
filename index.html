Lower Body
✓
Leg Press
Machine · push through heels
3 × 10
What it is: A reclined machine where you push a weighted footplate away with both feet.

How: Sit back flat on pad, feet shoulder-width on platform. Bend knees to bring plate toward you, push back out — don't lock knees at top.

Works: Quads, glutes, hamstrings.
💡 Lower back must stay pressed into the seat throughout. If it arches — weight is too heavy.
✓
Seated Leg Curl
Machine · curl heels toward seat
3 × 12
What it is: Sit upright and curl your lower legs downward against a padded bar on your shins.

How: Pad just above ankles. Curl legs down as far as comfortable — like tucking heels under seat. Squeeze, then slowly return.

Works: Hamstrings (back of thighs).
💡 Go slow on the return — that's where most muscle benefit happens.
✓
Glute Bridge
Floor · squeeze bum at the top
3 × 12
What it is: Lie on your back and lift your hips to the ceiling.

How: On back, knees bent, feet flat hip-width apart. Push through heels, squeeze glutes, lift hips until straight line from shoulders to knees. Hold 1 sec, lower slowly.

Works: Glutes, hamstrings, core.
💡 Actively squeeze your bum at the top — not your back. Feet too far out? Bring them closer in.
Upper Body
✓
Lat Pulldown
Machine · pull bar down to chest
3 × 10
What it is: Seated cable machine — pull a wide bar from above your head down to your chest.

How: Grip bar wider than shoulders. Pull down to upper chest, lean slightly back, squeeze shoulder blades. Let bar rise slowly.

Works: Lats (sides of back), biceps.
💡 Pull with your elbows, not your hands. Imagine tucking elbows into back pockets.
✓
Chest Press Machine
Machine · push handles forward
3 × 10
What it is: Seated machine — push two handles forward from chest height.

How: Back flat on pad. Grip handles at chest, elbows out. Push forward until arms almost extended. Slowly return.

Works: Chest, front shoulders, triceps.
💡 Keep shoulders down — they tend to shrug up as you push. Consciously keep them away from ears.
✓
DB Shoulder Press
Two dumbbells · press straight overhead
3 × 10
What it is: Dumbbell in each hand, press from shoulder height straight overhead.

How: Dumbbells at shoulder height, palms forward. Brace core. Press both straight up. Lower slowly.

Works: Shoulders, triceps.
💡 Tighten tummy before pressing. Feeling it in lower back? Go lighter or sit down.
Core
✓
Plank
Hold body in a straight line
3 × 30s
What it is: Static hold on forearms and toes in a straight line.

How: Elbows under shoulders. Straight line head to heels. Hold without hips sagging or rising. Breathe normally.

Works: Core, shoulders, glutes.
💡 Start with 15s if 30s is too hard. Perfect 15s beats saggy 30s every time.

Rules & Timeline
Especially important near 40
📋
Golden Rules
🥩Hit 90g+ protein daily — protects muscle in a deficit
🐢Never run hard — always conversational pace
😴Rest days mandatory — muscles grow during rest
👟Good running shoes — non-negotiable
⚖️Increase weights every 2 weeks — form first, always
🚦Soreness = OK. Sharp/joint pain = stop immediately
🌙7–9 hrs sleep — cortisol near 40 affects fat storage
🔄Stretch after every session. Foam roll when sore
💊Vitamins: ask doctor about D3, B12, Magnesium
Goal Timeline
Wk 1–4Foundation
Build habit — showing up counts
Stamina slowly improves
Wk 5–8Progress
Fat loss and energy shift visible
Runs getting longer and easier
Wk 9–12Results
Continuous jogging possible
Visible toning and fitness
Tracking & Adjustments
⚖️Weigh weekly — same time, same conditions (morning)
📉No loss after 2 weeks → cut 100–150 kcal from carbs/fat
⚡Workouts suffering → add 20–30g carbs (sweet potato)
🥗Always hungry → add more non-starchy veg (unlimited!)
📏Measure waist + hips every 4 weeks — scale can lie
